## Introduction to HertzifyOS
HertzifyOS is a custom Android ROM focused on providing a clean, aesthetic, and high-performance experience. Our project aims to simplify the Android ecosystem by removing unnecessary bloat while maintaining core functionalities.

### Our Philosophy
- **Security**: Utilizing the **Settings.Secure** framework for robust data storage logic.
- **Core Experience**: Crafting "Core GApps" packages to include only essential Google services.
