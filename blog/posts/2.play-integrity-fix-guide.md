# HertzifyOS – Play Integrity Fix Guide

This guide will help you fix **Google Play Integrity** and pass all available integrity levels on your HertzifyOS device:

- ✅ **Basic Integrity**
- ✅ **Device Integrity**
- ✅ **Strong Integrity**

Follow the steps carefully to ensure your device remains "clean" in Google's ecosystem 👇

---

## Passing Basic Integrity

This is the fundamental level required for most banking and essential applications to function.

1. Open **Settings**.
2. Navigate to **Hertzify Settings → Misc → Play Integrity Fix**.
3. Tap **Fetch/Import PIF**.

**Done!**  
After completing these steps, your device should now **pass Basic Integrity**.

---

## Passing Strong Integrity

To pass **Strong Integrity**, a **valid keybox XML** is strictly required.

### Steps to Follow

1. Obtain a **valid keybox XML** from a trusted source.  
   > ⚠️ **Note:** HertzifyOS **does not provide** keyboxes. You must find a valid source yourself.
2. Open **Hertzify Settings → Misc → Tricky Store**.
3. Tap **Import Keybox** and load your valid keybox file.
4. Open **Manage Target Apps**:
   - Search for **Google Play Services** and **Google Play Store**.
   - Select the appropriate mode:
     - **Leaf?** → If your device has a **working TEE**.
     - **Gen!** → If your device has a **broken TEE** *(Common on OnePlus, Motorola, etc.)*.
5. Recheck your **Play Integrity** status.

You should now **pass Strong Integrity**!

---

## Passing up to Device Integrity (Basic + Device)

- Previously, spoofing the **Play Store** allowed passing **Device Integrity**, but Google has now **patched** this method.
- If Google **soft-bans** keyboxes, a keybox that previously passed **Strong Integrity** may still allow your device to pass **up to Device Integrity**.

---

## Notes & Warnings

- Google may change Play Integrity behavior **at any time** — enjoy the functionality while it lasts.
- **Do not abuse keyboxes** for "flexing." Use them **only when necessary** for your essential apps.
